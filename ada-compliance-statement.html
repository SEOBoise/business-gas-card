ADA Compliance Statement

At Business Gas Card, we are committed to ensuring that our website, www.business-gas-card.com, is accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive digital experience that meets or exceeds accessibility standards in compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1.

Accessibility Features

Our website incorporates various accessibility features to enhance usability for all visitors:

  • Keyboard Navigation: Visitors can navigate the website using a keyboard, without the need for a mouse.
  • Text Alternatives: Images and multimedia content include descriptive text (ALT tags) for screen readers.
  • Readable Fonts: Text is presented in clear, legible fonts to improve readability.
  • Adjustable Text Size: Visitors can increase or decrease the font size using browser settings or assistive technology.
  • Color Contrast: We ensure sufficient contrast between text and background to make content readable for individuals with visual impairments.
  • Accessible Forms: Forms on our website are designed with clear labels and instructions for all users.

Ongoing Efforts

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing process, and we are continually improving our website to ensure it remains user-friendly for all individuals. We regularly:

  • Conduct accessibility audits to identify and resolve potential issues.
  • Stay updated on evolving ADA and WCAG standards.
  • Incorporate feedback from users to enhance accessibility.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any difficulties accessing content on our website or have suggestions for improving accessibility, please contact us. We are happy to assist and appreciate your feedback.
